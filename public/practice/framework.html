What the framework covers

Four stages. One connected system.

Each stage addresses a specific part of the patient journey that most EHRs either don't automate or don't connect to your front-facing channels.

01

Getting Found & Booked

Voice agent for inbound calls, missed call recovery, Google Business Profile booking, website booking — 24/7 without staff.

02

Before the Appointment

Automated intake, provider briefing, team routing for multi-provider practices. Patient arrives prepared, provider arrives informed.

03

After the Appointment

Review request, referral prompt, auto-rebook for package patients, onsite nudge for everyone else. All automated, nothing manual.

04

Reactivation

For patients who don't rebook: a structured Day 7 / Day 14 / Day 30 sequence that stops automatically when they book.
