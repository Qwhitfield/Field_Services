What this audit does

We follow the patient journey from click to chair.

This isn't about whether your website looks good. It's about whether the entire path from "I found you online" to "I booked an appointment" is connected, automated, and working without a human babysitting it.

Website as a conversion tool

Is your site set up to convert visitors, or just inform them? We look at booking entry points, friction, and clarity of next steps.

No-show and drop-off handling

5

Visibility into what's actually working
